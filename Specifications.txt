DATA REQUIREMENTS

Note that these requirements are still subject to change or modification.
1. Photo
  - must be a portrait where subject keeps a straight face
2. Full name
  - First name
  - Last name
  - other names
3. Date of Birth
  - Month
  - Day
  - Year
4. Place of birth
5. Nationality
6. Sex
7. Passport Number
8. Passport's date of issue
9. Passport's expiry date


FUNCTIONALITY SPECIFICATIONS
- Blockchain technology should be implemented to ensure that fraudulent activity is kept at a minimum.
- Users should be able to edit their data having gone through a heavily blockchain-based authentication process.
- standardization acceptable by a strong majority of countries.
